A couple things to keep in mind…

  1. Picture Controls/Styles are applied to JPEG images, so the image quality has to be set to either JPEG or RAW+JPEG.
  2. Once a JPEG photo has been taken with a certain Picture Control/Style, you can’t go back and reverse it to something else. So if you shoot a JPEG photo in black and white, it will be black and white forever. I recommend shooting in RAW+JPEG, that way you have a RAW image that retains the color in case you want to reverse it later on.
